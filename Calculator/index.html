Welcome to My Calculator Site!

Hi Everyone!

As a pure mathematics graduate student, I was a teaching assistant and had to grade quizzes and worksheets on solving quadratic equations and system of linear equations. I would do these exercises first to find their answers and then grade my students' work. Now I wanted to build a site that would make this easier, but how?

After learning JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and getting exposed to AWS technologies during an online training, I researched how to host on an AWS S3 bucket. Then I constructed this sites' pages locally that would solve any quadratic equation or system of equations it was given! After uploading these files to an AWS S3 bucket, anyone can use this site and cut their grading load in half!
